Our Practice

Landscape & hardscape
experts since 1997

For more than two decades, Reda Brothers Landscape has set the standard for excellence within the landscape and hardscape industry throughout Northern New Jersey.

Based in Flanders, New Jersey, we specialize in high-end landscape design, hardscape construction, and outdoor living environments — custom poolscapes, waterfalls, architectural stonework, outdoor kitchens, and complete estate transformations. Every project reflects our commitment to precision, premium materials, and timeless design.

As a family-owned firm led by John Reda, we bring a level of personal investment that larger operations simply cannot match. When you work with Reda Brothers, you work directly with the craftsmen who oversee every stone, every joint, every detail.

How We Work

From first conversation
to final walkthrough

1

Discovery

We visit your property, listen to your vision, assess the site, and discuss scope, materials, and budget expectations — no obligation, completely free.

2

Design

Custom design plans with material selections, 3D renderings when needed, engineering specifications, and a detailed project timeline.

3

Construction

Our crews execute with meticulous precision — from excavation and drainage to every cut stone and finished joint. Open communication throughout.

4

Delivery

Final walkthrough, quality inspection, and handover. We ensure your complete satisfaction and provide care guidance for lasting beauty.
